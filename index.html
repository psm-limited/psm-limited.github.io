Liquid roofing

Liquid coating systems have been used on flat roofing schemes over the past 50 years. The materials are installed using cold application which helps to reduce the health and safety risks away from the design.

The application process allows the systems to be applied onto most surfaces including asphalt, felt, metal and single ply membranes. This installation makes the liquid coating system very versatile and reduces the cost of removal and disposal of the old roof. Liquid coating roof systems are extremely flexible and can be incorporated using insulation to upgrade thermal performance and are extremely effective when detailing around awkward details. These systems come with labour and material backed guarantees, to ensure your flat roofs are water tight for up to a period of 25 Years.
